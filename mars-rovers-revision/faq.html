Frequently Asked Questions

General Mars Questions

Why do we send rovers instead of astronauts?

Rovers can operate in Mars' harsh environment for years, while human missions require much more complex life support systems and carry greater risks.

How long does it take to reach Mars?

The journey takes 6-8 months depending on planetary alignment at launch.

Rover Specific Questions

How do rovers survive Martian winters?

Rovers use radioactive power sources and strategically park on slopes to maximize sunlight exposure for solar-powered components.

Can the rovers return to Earth?

No, current rovers are designed as permanent Mars residents. Future missions plan to retrieve samples collected by Perseverance.
