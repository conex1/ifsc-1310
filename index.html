Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is very easy to describe as the article puts up. It is the perspective to put your content first, instead of trying to make the website future proof for better devices, and leaving old devices in more or less the dust. It's a way better way of designing a website. There's no point to your website if it's just pretty and stuff and works. The only thing that brings people to websites is content. Either through videos like youtube, up to date news like news websites, or asking and answering questions like forums. They need to host a REAL reason to come. https://www.sitepoint.com/progressive-enhancement-1-html/
